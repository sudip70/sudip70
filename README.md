## 🤓 Did you know?

<details>
<summary><b>🏒 Why January babies dominate the NHL...</b> <i>(click to expand)</i></summary>
<br>

Kids with birthdays in the **first quarter of the year** are more likely to make it to the National Hockey League.

Why? It's called the **Relative Age Effect**. Youth hockey groups kids by age using a cutoff (usually January 1). So kids born January-March are slightly older, bigger, and stronger than their peers growing up... and that early advantage snowballs all the way to the pros. 🧊🥅

*Moral of the story: sometimes the biggest advantage is just showing up early.* (Works for data pipelines too.)

</details>
